In The Hole by Alton Brown

In The Hole is a recipe devised by acclaimed television food expert Alton Brown and delivered to Twitter User @HTHRFLWRS in a dream.

Ingredients

  • 1 Packet of Earl Grey Tea
  • 1 Packet of Peppermint Tea
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Brew 1 Mug of Earl Grey Tea
  2. Brew 1 Mug of Peppermint Tea
  3. Pour the Earl Grey Tea into a Large Bowl
  4. Pour the Peppermint Tea into the Large Bowl
  5. Take special care to ensure you've followed the order, it is Vital
  6. Salt Vigorously
