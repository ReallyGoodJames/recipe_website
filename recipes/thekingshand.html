The Kings Hand Recipe

Originating in Twitter User @Neo Cannolialist's dreams. The Kings Hand is a dreamworld delicacy.

Ingredients

  • 1 Pound of M&Ms
  • 1 Tube of your favorite Cookie Dough
  • Ovensafe Foodgrade Silicone Plastique Mold
  • Greek Salad

Steps

  1. First, you will need to make a mold of a hand. For this I used food grade silicon putty safe up to 400 degrees.
  2. Make M&M cookie dough. I just took some recipe off online
  3. Fill your mold with the dough
  4. Put the m&ms for the nails and knuckles in first
  5. Set the oven to a lower temp than the recipe requires (mine was 325 vs 375) and bake for like 2-3 times as long
  6. Take it out and scoop out dough for the base and to make it hollow
  7. Flatten the scooped out stuff into a thin sheet and put in the oven to bake, along with the hand again. I dunno, I did like 10 minutes. Raise the temp back up to bc now you want it hot and baked
  8. Take it out and freeze it! Gotta be cool and solid to demold. Meanwhile chop up the Greek salad.
  9. When it is solid remove from the mold, cut around the hand to remove the overhanging edges
